React.js Three.js Flask WebRTC OpenCV PyTorch

An educational rhythmic and song generation game that lets players learn and practice American Sign Language (ASL).

Players can practice 4 different signs at a time (ex. A B C D) that map to the notes in a song. Their score is based on getting the sign correct at the correct time as well as how many they get correctly in a row.
